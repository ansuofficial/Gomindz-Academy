Executive Summary Gomindz Academy

Mission Statement

To build an Ed-Tech community that will pioneer and démocratise Data Science, Artificial Intelligence and Programming to bring ideas to life.

Vision Statement

On a journey to build a Digital Campus for Data Engineers, Developers and Creators to stimulate regional Economic Growth.

Objectives

1. To pioneer and démocratise Data Science and Programming to the masses.

3. To mitigate the unemployment rate by connecting our community to global markets.

3. To facilitate hands-on training on software design and Development.

4. Bridge the gender-gap balance of this male dominant sector.

5. To nurture first principle thinking approach in solving problems (Engineering Ingenuity).

5-Year Strategic Plan

1. To achieve 70% employability or engagement of all our students in the community by creating fellowship/incubation programs.

2. To enhance Digital Inclusion on tech-related careers to boost the awareness of Digital literacy.

3. To provide tailored programs that will re-skill and up-skill the workforce to world class expertise.

4. To pioneer and démocratise the Evolution of Data Science and Analytics Education in the system.

5. To enforce and foster programming knowledge to the youngsters for future prospects.
